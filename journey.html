My Journey in Cybersecurity So Far

From curiosity about data protection and compliance to hands-on projects in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), this page documents how my path into cybersecurity has evolved over time.

How it Started

I began exploring cybersecurity through compliance work, policy reviews, and understanding frameworks like HIPAA and other regulatory requirements. Over time, I realized that I was especially drawn to Governance, Risk, and Compliance because it connects technical security to real-world risk and business impact.

Key Milestones

  • Participated in departmental HIPAA compliance activities.
  • Completed foundational learning such as Google Cybersecurity material.
  • Earned CompTIA Security+ as a baseline technical certification.
  • Committed to a 6-month intensive GRC training while building portfolio projects.
  • Obtained the ISACA CISA certification to strengthen my audit and control skills.

Where I’m Headed

My goal is to apply GRC principles in environments like healthcare and other highly regulated industries, helping organizations understand their risks, define controls, and build repeatable, auditable processes for security and compliance.
